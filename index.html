Welcome to CodAid

Here you will find useful tips and ready-to-use Lua scripts for your Roblox game development journey.

CodAid is a platform dedicated to helping developers of all skill levels create amazing experiences in Roblox Studio. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your scripting skills, we provide well-documented, pre-written scripts and explanations to speed up your development process.

In this site, you will find different categories such as:

  • Basic Code: Learn the essential Lua scripting concepts for Roblox.
  • Advanced Code: Explore more complex mechanics and game features.
  • Character Settings: Customize player attributes like movement speed, health, and animations.
  • Predone Code: Use ready-made scripts for common gameplay mechanics such as sprinting and crouching.

If you need specific help, feel free to reach out by leaving a comment or asking a question. We're here to support your game development journey!
